Historical Places

Nelson Mandela House

Nelson Mandela's house, especially the one in Soweto, Johannesburg, is rich with historical significance. Here are three interesting facts about it: Symbol of resistance, preserved as a museum and symbol of reconciliation.

Constitutional Hill

Constitution Hill in Johannesburg is a significant historical and cultural site in South Africa. Here are three interesting facts about it: Former prison complex, transformation into a symbol of democracy, cultural and educational center.

Hector Pieterson Memorial

The Hector Pieterson Memorial is a significant historical site located in Orlando West, Soweto, South Africa. Here's more about it: Commemoration of the Soweto Uprising, Symbol of Resistance and Cultural Landmark.
